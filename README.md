4. The EstatePulse icon will now appear on your phone like a regular app!

## üìä How to Use

### 1. Daily Logging
- Every evening, tap **"Log New Report"**.
- Select the **Date** and the **Source** (Owner or Broker).
- Use the **+** and **-** buttons to enter the number of new properties found today.
- Tap **"Archive Daily Report"** to save.

### 2. Analytics
- The **Performance** tab shows you a live scale of our growth.
- Use the **Line Graphs** to see which days were our most productive.
- Use the **Horizontal Bars** to see which property categories (like Showroom Sale or Office Lease) are currently our biggest inventories.

### 3. Data Safety & Export
- **Data Storage:** All data you enter is stored **locally on your device**. It will not be lost if you refresh or close the browser.
- **Excel Sync:** At the end of every week, go to the dashboard and tap **"Excel Export"**. This will download a `.csv` file which you can open in Excel to merge into our master database.
